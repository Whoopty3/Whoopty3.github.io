Quick glimpse at our customers favorites

Classic Chocolate Pistachio
Layers of espresso-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, and cocoa powder. The traditional version, often flavored with a hint of Marsala wine or coffee liqueur. A richer take on the original, with layers of chocolate cream, chocolate shavings, and sometimes a chocolate liqueur or ganache added to the mix. Pistachio paste or cream is mixed into the mascarpone, giving it a nutty, slightly sweet flavor, and it's often topped with crushed pistachios for added texture.
